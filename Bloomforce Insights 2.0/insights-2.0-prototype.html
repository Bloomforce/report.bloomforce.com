The career ladder — entry to architect

The role with the depth to benchmark cleanly today. Entry, Mid and Senior are measured; Principal and Architect are modeled until the data fills in. Per-application breakdowns (Willow, Beaker…) return once each has the sample to stand on its own.

⚕️
A clinical license resets the ladder
Willow / pharmacy build roles that require a PharmD or RPh run ~$144k (typically $128k–$166k) — about 29% above the ~$112k non-licensed equivalent, and past a generic Senior. The same premium shows up wherever a build role needs a clinical credential — RN for clinical modules, RHIA/RHIT for HIM.
